About Me

Hello! My name is Rikita Hirpara and I am currently a freshman at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign majoring in computer science. My hometown is Carol Stream, IL and I went to Glenbard North high school as a part of the 2013 graduating class. Throughout high school, I volunteered at Alexian Brothers Hospital and Windsor Park Manor. I also participated in clubs such as Model United Nations and Indian Student Association and was a vice president for the National Honors Society.

At UIUC, I want to continue my interest in student run organizations. I started off by getting involved in Women in Computer Science, Association for Computing Machinery, and Society of Women Engineers. I got experience with Visual Basic over the summer and now, I am learning Java in CS125. I am looking forward to gaining more knowledge in the Computer Science field and learning new programming languages.

If you want to know more, take a second to look at my resume!
